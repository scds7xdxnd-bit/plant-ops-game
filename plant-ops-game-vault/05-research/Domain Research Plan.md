# Domain Research Plan

#### Purpose

This note tracks the research needed to make the game feel grounded without overbuilding the simulation.

#### Research Streams

| Stream | Questions |
|---|---|
| Customer discovery | How do industrial customers phrase improvement requests? |
| Marketing translation | How are customer signals converted into market opportunities? |
| Product and process design | What requirements commonly get lost in translation? |
| Plant operations | What constraints dominate real improvement projects? |
| Finance | How do plants evaluate capex, downtime, and margin tradeoffs? |
| Safety and compliance | What risks should be represented carefully? |
| Education and training | What should a learner understand after one scenario? |

#### Interview Targets

- Plant managers
- Process engineers
- Product managers in industrial businesses
- Technical sales or application engineers
- Quality managers
- Maintenance and reliability engineers
- Operations finance leads

#### Evidence Types

- Interview notes
- Public case studies
- Textbook examples
- Industry reports
- Simplified engineering assumptions
- Expert review comments

#### Research Output Format

Each useful finding should become one of:

- Scenario seed
- Metric assumption
- Stakeholder behavior pattern
- Failure mode
- Open question
- Decision record
